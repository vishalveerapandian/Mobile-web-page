About ASIFA 26

Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability: Shaping the Future Ahead

Dates: 15th, 16th & 17th January 2026

Venue: ITM Skills University, Kharghar, Navi Mumbai

The ITM International Research Conference (ASIFA 26) is a prestigious three-day academic event that brings together researchers, scholars, industry professionals, and students to explore cutting-edge developments in Artificial Intelligence and Sustainability. This conference provides a comprehensive platform for knowledge exchange, networking, and collaborative research in management and technology domains.

Conference Schedule & Highlights:

  • Student Research Conference - 15th January 2026: A dynamic platform for undergraduate and postgraduate students to present their innovative research projects and gain valuable feedback from academic experts
  • Doctoral Colloquium - 16th January 2026: An exclusive forum for doctoral scholars to discuss their research progress, methodologies, and findings with experienced faculty members and industry mentors
  • International Research Conference - 17th January 2026: The flagship event featuring keynote addresses from renowned international speakers, paper presentations, and panel discussions on AI and sustainability in management
  • Publication Opportunities: Selected papers will be published in ISBN proceedings and submitted to Scopus-indexed journals, providing valuable academic credentials to authors

Expected Footfall: 1000+ participants including management students, faculty members, researchers, and industry professionals
